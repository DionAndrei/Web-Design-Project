Who We Are?

MasterSilog is not just a website; it's your go-to destination for a mini karendirya adventure. We've seamlessly blended the nostalgic charm of a local eatery with the convenience of an online platform. From savory bites to sweet treats, MasterSilog is your virtual haven for a diverse array of delectable delights. our passionate team of developers brought MasterSilog to life: Cedrick James Soriano, Dion Andrei Bercasio, John Arvin Samatra, Rein Jea Bacsain, Neil Aldrin Terrible. As you explore Master Ace, know that each element has been crafted with love and dedication by our dynamic team. Welcome to our digital mini karendirya Master Ace, where the joy of dining meets the ease of the online world.
